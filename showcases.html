COMING SOON: Project Showcases

Throughout this academic year, Lilly Leadership Institute students from Cohorts XI, XII, and XIII have been designing projects to showcase AI's potential in customer service. Video summaries of each group's progress will be added to this page soon!


About the Showcase

Hosted by the Lilly Leadership Institute, this showcase features cross-disciplinary teams of students presenting innovative approaches to using AI for enhancing customer engagement. Each project applies AI technology to address customer needs and improve user experiences.

Event Details

Date: April 11, 2025

Location: McVey Data Science Building Atrium

Goals of the Showcase

Enhance customer-centric innovation using AI-driven solutions.
Promote cross-disciplinary collaboration for customer impact.
Prepare students for a rapidly evolving business environment.
Engage with industry leaders on future business challenges.
Develop customer-focused problem-solving skills through AI.
